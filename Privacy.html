🥐 1. Information We Collect

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy describes how we handle information when you interact with our platform, events, café vlogs, and celebration features. Depending on your activity, we may collect:

  • Personal identifiers – name, email address, username, or similar contact details (only when voluntarily provided).

  • Multimedia content – if you upload event photos or café vlog materials, we may collect metadata such as timestamps and image descriptions (only with explicit consent).
  • Cookies & similar tech – to remember preferences and analyze anonymized trends.

We never collect sensitive personal information without clear notice and your affirmative consent.

🥐 2. How We Use Your Information

Your information helps us create a personalized, secure, and seamless experience across our services. Uses include:

  • Providing, operating, and improving our event updates, celebration galleries, and café vlog features.
  • Communicating with you regarding policy changes, security alerts, or support messages.

  • Complying with legal obligations and enforcing our terms.

🥐 Mood & Bites promise: We will never sell your personal information to third parties. Any data sharing is limited, transparent, and required by service operation or law.

🥐 3. Sharing & Disclosure

We may share your data only in the following limited scenarios:

  • Service providers & partners – trusted third parties who assist with hosting, analytics (e.g., anonymized metrics), or customer support. They are bound by strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal compliance – when required by law, subpoena, or governmental request to protect our rights, safety, or property.
  • Business transfers – in the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your information may be transferred, but we will notify you beforehand.
  • With your explicit consent – for any other purpose disclosed at the time of collection.

All shared data is minimized and pseudonymized whenever possible.

🥐 4. Data Security & Retention

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against accidental loss, unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. Our security protocols include encryption (TLS), regular security audits, and access controls.

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. For event photos or vlog content, we keep your materials active as long as you maintain an account or until you request deletion.

📌 Retention period summary: Account info – until account closure; analytics logs – up to 24 months in aggregated form; support records – 3 years.

🥐 5. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location (including GDPR, CCPA, and similar frameworks), you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access – obtain confirmation whether we process your data and request a copy.
  • Right to Rectification – correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Right to Erasure (Right to be forgotten) – request deletion of your data under certain conditions.
  • Right to Restrict Processing – limit how we use your data.
  • Right to Data Portability – receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to Object – opt-out of certain processing (e.g., direct marketing).
  • Right to Withdraw Consent – where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw anytime.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information in Section 8. We will respond within 30 days.

🥐 6. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve functionality, analyze performance, and provide a customized experience. You can manage cookie preferences via your browser settings. Types of cookies include:

  • Essential cookies: required for navigation and basic features (security, authentication).
  • Analytics/performance cookies: help us count visits and traffic sources to measure site performance (e.g., Google Analytics anonymized data).
  • Functionality cookies: remember your preferences (language, region) for a seamless return.

By using our services, you consent to our use of cookies as described. You may disable non-essential cookies, but some features may be limited.
